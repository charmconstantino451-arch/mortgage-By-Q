# Design Breakdown: "MORTGAGE Website Design"
**Source**: [Dribbble Shot by Muhammad Shofiuddoula / Zeyox Studio](https://dribbble.com/shots/24827160-MORTGAGE-Website-design)

---

## Overview / First Impression

This design is **bold, editorial, and unexpectedly raw**. It throws out the soft, reassuring pastels typical of mortgage brands and instead uses a **high-contrast, neo-brutalist palette** of electric lime-green (`#C8F04B`-range) against near-black dark forest/charcoal. The result feels more like a premium architecture or luxury real estate brand than a traditional lender. Think: a modern design agency that decided to do mortgages.

**Color Contrast:** This is neo-brutalist — the lime-green on dark green creates electric energy. Very unexpected for mortgages.

## Typography

| Element | Style | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Hero Headline** | All-caps, ultra-bold, condensed display serif | Feels like `Monument Extended` or `Clash Display` |
| **Section Headlines** | All-caps, bold, condensed geometric sans | Very tight letter-spacing, poster-like |
| **Footer Brand Name** | Ultra-wide display, possibly custom or `Anton` / `Bebas Neue` style | Fills entire screen width |
| **Body Text** | Very small, clean sans-serif | Muted grey/white, minimal — almost invisible deliberately |
| **Nav Links** | Small-caps, light weight sans-serif | Clean and airy |
| **Card Labels** | All-caps, medium weight | Clear hierarchy |

**Typography Character:**
- Condensed + bold = editorial authority
- All-caps throughout = confident, direct, premium
- Very small body text = trusts the visuals and design to communicate, not text

## Logo Analysis

```
❈  MORTGAGE
```

- **Symbol**: `❈` (snowflake/asterisk — 8-pointed geometric star)
- **Brand Name**: `MORTGAGE` — all caps, clean modern sans-serif, medium weight
- **Lockup**: Icon + wordmark in horizontal line, very tight
- **Color adaptations seen**:
  - Dark on white/transparent nav
  - White on dark backgrounds
  - Dark on lime green footer

**Design Decision**: The `❈` symbol is a geometric marker that works in any size. It's minimal, modern, and geometric — not literal (no houses, keys, etc.). Very smart branding.

## Specific Techniques to Steal for Mortgage by Q

### High-Value / Directly Applicable:
1. **Section Color-Blocking**: Rotate backgrounds per section (cream → teal → gold → dark) instead of one uniform background. Each section feels like a new chapter.
2. **Lifestyle Photography Hero**: Full-bleed warm photo with bold text overlay. People in the photo should be aspirational but not stock-photo perfect.
3. **Circular Avatar + Arrow CTA**: The inline "faces + arrow button" CTA is extremely modern. Replaces the standard big blue button.
4. **Typographic Wallpaper Footer**: Use `MORTGAGE BY Q` at massive scale in the footer as a brand statement.
5. **Icon Cards on Dark BG**: Dark card grid with circular colored icons for each service (Purchase, Refinance, HELOC, Investment).
6. **"Why Choose Us" Warm Section**: Terracotta/gold warm-toned section with a real family photo — humanizes the brand powerfully.

### Adapt (not copy):
7. **Lime Green → Q Gold**: Replace the electric lime with Mortgage by Q's primary accent (warm gold, teal, or sage) — same energy, different personality.
8. **The `❈` Logo Symbol → A `Q`-forward Mark**: Study how the geometric star acts as both punctuation and icon. Q can do the same.
9. **Service Cards**: 4-column card grid maps perfectly to Q's core services (Purchase, Refinance, HELOC, VA).

## What This Design Says About Brand Positioning

This design says: **"We are not your parents' mortgage company."**

It is a direct, confident brand statement made entirely through visual language — not copy. The lime green alone communicates: *we are different, we are modern, we have a perspective.*

For **Mortgage by Q**, the equivalent would be: use the brand name and visual identity to signal that Q is in a completely different category from the boring institutional lenders — without having to say it explicitly.
